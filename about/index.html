自 2022 年 9 月加入游戏行业。 因对“道家哲学的游戏化表达”感兴趣，不断学习，希望能够形成自己独特的游戏设计哲学。 受陈星汉（Jenova Chen）启发，尝试构建情绪化游戏设计系统（Emotional Game Design System, EGDS）作为设计方法论。 后受荣格的阴影（Shadow）和自性化（Individuation）概念启发，希望为了自己，和如我一般的人，创造发现阴影和成为自己的机会。 Join the game industry in September 2022. Driven by a deep interest in the gamified expression of Taoist philosophy, I continue to explore and learn, aspiring to develop my own distinctive philosophy of game design. Inspired by Jenova Chen, I am developing the Emotional Game Design System (EGDS) — a methodological framework for crafting emotional player experiences. Guided by Carl Jung’s concept of the Shadow and Individuation, I seek to create opportunities — for myself and for those like me — to discover and embrace our hidden selves, moving toward wholeness through games.
